NATIONAL GEOGRAPHIC SOCIETY CHAIRMAN'S AWARD

The National Geographic Society Chairman's Award is one of the organization's highest honors, given to individuals who have made exceptional contributions to exploration, science, education, and storytelling. The award recognizes visionary leaders whose work embodies the spirit and mission of the National Geographic Society.

REASON FOR ELECTION

Fei-Fei Li was honored with the National Geographic Society Chairman's Award for her pioneering contributions to artificial intelligence, particularly in the realm of computer vision, and for her efforts to promote ethical and inclusive AI.

ONE OF THE "100 MOST INFLUENTIAL PEOPLE" BY TIME MAGAZINE

Time Magazine's "100 Most Influential People" is an annual list that highlights individuals from various fields who have made substantial contributions and wield significant influence on global affairs, culture, and society.

REASON FOR ELECTION

Fei-Fei Li was included in Time Magazine's "100 Most Influential People" list due to her groundbreaking contributions to artificial intelligence, particularly in the field of computer vision, and her influential leadership roles in academia and industry.

03

CO-DIRECTOR OF THE STANFORD HUMAN-CENTERED AI INSTITUTE (HAI)

The Stanford Human-Centered AI Institute (HAI) is a multidisciplinary institute at Stanford University dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence (AI) research, education, policy, and practice. Founded in 2019, HAI aims to promote AI technologies that are not only powerful and efficient but also ethical, fair, and beneficial to humanity.

REASON FOR ELECTION

Fei-Fei Li was elected as Co-Director of Stanford HAI for her groundbreaking AI research, dedication to ethical and human-centered AI, influential leadership, commitment to diversity, and significant educational contributions. These align with HAI's mission to develop AI that benefits society.
