INSTITUTE OF ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERS (IEEE)

The IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. It has over 400,000 members in more than 160 countries and is a leading authority on various fields, including electrical engineering, electronics, and computer science.

REASON FOR ELECTION

Fei-Fei Li was recognized for her outstanding contributions to the field of computer vision and artificial intelligence, particularly her work on the ImageNet project and her significant advancements in deep learning and visual recognition technologies.

02

"PIONEER IN AI" AWARD FROM THE WORLD ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE CONFERENCE (WAIC)

The WAIC is an international event that gathers leading experts, researchers, industry leaders, and policymakers in the field of artificial intelligence. The conference aims to showcase the latest advancements in AI, foster collaboration, and discuss the future directions of AI technology and its societal impacts.

REASON FOR ELECTION

The “Pioneer in AI” award recognizes individuals who have made groundbreaking contributions to the field of artificial intelligence. The award honors visionaries whose work has significantly advanced AI technology and has had a profound impact on research, industry, and society.

03

ATHENA AWARD FOR ACADEMIC LEADERSHIP FROM THE ATHENA PINNACLE AWARDS

The Athena Pinnacle Awards are presented by Athena, an organization dedicated to advancing women in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) through mentoring, education, recognition, and leadership training. The awards celebrate individuals and organizations that have made significant contributions to the promotion of women in the STEM fields.

REASON FOR ELECTION

Fei-Fei Li was honored with the Athena Award for Academic Leadership in recognition of her outstanding contributions to academia and her leadership in promoting diversity and inclusion in STEM.
