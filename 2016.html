J.K. AGGARWAL PRIZE FROM IAPR

The prize is named in honor of Dr. J.K. Aggarwal, a renowned researcher in the field of pattern recognition and computer vision. He has made significant contributions to the development of these areas and has been a prominent figure in the academic community.

REASON FOR ELECTION

Fei-Fei Li was awarded the J.K. Aggarwal Prize for her pioneering work in computer vision, particularly for her contributions to the development of large-scale image datasets and deep learning algorithms for visual recognition.

ONE OF THE "PEOPLE WHO MATTER" BY TIME MAGAZINE

Time Magazine is a leading global news magazine known for its annual lists that highlight influential individuals, such as the "Time 100" and "People Who Matter." These lists recognize people who have made significant impacts in various fields, including politics, science, technology, entertainment, and social activism.

REASON FOR ELECTION

Fei-Fei Li was recognized for her pioneering work in artificial intelligence, particularly in the field of computer vision, and for her influential role in advancing AI research and promoting its ethical use.

ONE OF THE "40 UNDER 40" BY FORTUNE MAGAZINE

Fortune Magazine's "40 Under 40" is an annual list that highlights the most influential young leaders in business, spanning various industries including technology, finance, healthcare, media, and more. The list celebrates innovators, disruptors, and visionaries under the age of 40 who are making significant impacts in their fields.

REASON FOR ELECTION

Fei-Fei Li was included in Fortune's "40 Under 40" list due to her pioneering work in artificial intelligence, particularly in computer vision, and her influential roles in academia and industry.
